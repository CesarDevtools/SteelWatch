Field Manual

🎮 Battle Controls

Master the steel to survive the Arena:

  • [Q] Basic Attack: A reliable strike. Moderate damage with a very low chance of being blocked. Your bread and butter.
  • [E] Sneak Attack: A high-risk gambit. Easily blocked by focused enemies. While it often deals low damage, a perfect strike grants a x3 Damage Multiplier.
  • [SPACEBAR] Parry: The ultimate defense. Timing is everything. Press it exactly when the enemy strikes to deflect the blow and stay alive.

⚔️ Combat Stances

Your chosen class modifies your attributes:

  • Tank: Massive HP, but strikes with reduced power.
  • Balanced: Versatile stats for any encounter.
  • Assassin: Fragile health, but lethal critical potential.

💀 Enemy Intel

Opponents follow an Aggressive Pattern. They prioritize damage unless staggered by your tactical strikes.
